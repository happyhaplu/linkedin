🔐 How to Connect Your LinkedIn Account

Follow these steps to safely extract your LinkedIn session cookie and connect your account:

⚠️ Important: Never share your LinkedIn cookies with untrusted sources. These cookies give access to your LinkedIn account.

Step 1: Login to LinkedIn

1. Open a new tab and go to linkedin.com

2. Log in with your email and password (complete any 2FA if required)

3. Make sure you're logged in successfully

Step 2: Open Developer Tools

While on LinkedIn.com:

• Windows/Linux: Press F12 or Ctrl + Shift + I

• Mac: Press Cmd + Option + I

• Or right-click anywhere and select "Inspect"

Step 3: Go to Application/Storage Tab

1. In the Developer Tools, click on the "Application" tab (Chrome) or "Storage" tab (Firefox)

2. In the left sidebar, expand "Cookies"

3. Click on https://www.linkedin.com

Step 4: Find the li_at Cookie

1. Look for a cookie named li_at in the list

2. Click on it to see the full value

3. The value looks something like this:

AQEDATg4MTk3NjAwAAABjVm9R8AAAAGNWcBLwAAAAC....(very long string)

Step 5: Copy the Cookie Value

1. Double-click on the li_at cookie value to select it

2. Copy it (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)

3. Go back to the LinkedIn Accounts page

4. Click "Connect Account" and paste the cookie value

Step 6: (Optional) Get JSESSIONID Cookie

For better stability, also copy the JSESSIONID cookie:

1. Find JSESSIONID in the same cookies list

2. Copy its value

3. Paste it when prompted in the connection form

✅ Security Tips:
  • Only use this on your own LinkedIn account
  • Never share these cookies publicly
  • Change your LinkedIn password if cookies are compromised
  • Cookies expire - you may need to reconnect accounts periodically

This is how HeyReach, Expandi, and other LinkedIn automation tools work.
